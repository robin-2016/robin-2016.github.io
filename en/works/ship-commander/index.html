Overview

Ship Commander wraps Linux CLI learning in a spaceship repair narrative: you are the commander of ARIA-7, and the only way to fix failing systems is to run real Linux commands. The course runs in a browser-embedded Alpine Linux environment powered by a v86 WASM x86 emulator — every command is real, not simulated. No installation required: unzip and double-click to launch on Mac or Windows. Course content is available via 知识星球 (a Chinese subscription knowledge platform); follow the WeChat Official Account for updates. Scan the QR codes below to join.
